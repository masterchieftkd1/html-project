Arm Workouts

Strong arms are essential for many daily activities. Here are some effective arm workouts to help you build strength and definition in your arms.

Bicep curls

To do a biceps curl with a dumbbell, hold a dumbbell with your palm facing upward. Slowly curl the weight up by bending your elbow, keeping your elbow close to your body. Then slowly lower the weight to the starting position.

Preacher Curl

Set up on the preacher bench, holding a dumbbell in each hand by your shoulders with your palms facing away from you. Lower the weights slowly until your arms are fully extended, then rotate your hands so your palms face towards you and curl the dumbbells back up to your shoulders.
